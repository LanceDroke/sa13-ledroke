Yellowstone National Park

About

Yellowstone National Park was established on March 1, 1872, making it the first national park in the world. It covers over 2.2 million acres across Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho. The park sits on top of a massive volcanic hotspot and contains more than 10,000 geothermal features including geysers, hot springs, and mud pots.

Featured Areas

  • Old Faithful and Upper Geyser Basin
  • Grand Prismatic Spring
  • Lamar Valley
  • Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone
