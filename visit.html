Hours of Operation

The park is open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Visitor center hours vary by season.

Visitor Center Season Hours
Old Faithful Visitor Center May - October 8:00am - 8:00pm
Old Faithful Visitor Center November - April 9:00am - 5:00pm
Canyon Visitor Center May - October 8:00am - 6:00pm
Mammoth Visitor Center Year-round 9:00am - 5:00pm

Tickets and Reservations

You can purchase your entrance pass at any park entrance gate or visitor center. A private vehicle pass costs $35 and is valid for 7 days. No reservation is needed for day visits, but camping and lodging must be booked in advance at recreation.gov.
