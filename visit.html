Getting Here

Yellowstone has five entrance gates. The nearest major airports are:

  • Jackson Hole Airport (JAC) — 60 miles south via the South Entrance
  • Bozeman Yellowstone International (BZN) — 90 miles north via the North Entrance
  • Idaho Falls Regional (IDA) — 110 miles west via the West Entrance

There is no public transit into the park. A personal vehicle or guided tour is required. Rental cars are available at all nearby airports.
